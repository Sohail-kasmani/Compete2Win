About us

Our aim is to develop a web portal for intercollege competition by which the student community will get more opportunities to compete and learn along with their peers from different colleges across the country This will drastically benefit the student community and help to narrow the difference between the opportunities a student of prestigious college receive as compared to a student studying in an average college.
